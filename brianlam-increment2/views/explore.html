Our Mission

Monomuse is dedicated to preserving, interpreting, and celebrating the rich tapestry of human creativity and innovation. We believe that art and culture belong to everyone, and we strive to create an inclusive space where visitors can discover, learn, and be inspired by the extraordinary achievements of humanity across time and cultures.

Who We Serve

Monomuse welcomes a diverse community of visitors, including:

  • Students and educators seeking enriching cultural experiences
  • Families looking for engaging and educational activities
  • Art enthusiasts and collectors
  • Researchers and scholars
  • Tourists exploring our city's cultural landmarks
  • Community members of all ages and backgrounds

Our History

Founded in 1987, Monomuse has been a cornerstone of cultural education and preservation for nearly four decades. What began as a small collection has grown into one of the region's premier institutions for art and culture.

Milestones

  • 1987: Monomuse officially opens its doors to the public with a collection of 500 artifacts
  • 1995: Expansion of the East Wing, doubling our exhibition space
  • 2003: Launch of our award-winning education program serving over 10,000 students annually
  • 2010: Digital archive initiative begins, making our collection accessible online
  • 2015: Opening of the Contemporary Art Gallery
  • 2020: Virtual tours and online programming launched during global pandemic
  • 2023: Monomuse welcomes its 5 millionth visitor
