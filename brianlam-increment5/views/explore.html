Our History

Founded in 1987. Started as a small museum, but grew in locations and reach over the years.

Milestones

  • 1987: Opened with 20 collection items
  • 2003: Started education programs
  • 2010: Website launched
  • 2015: Achieved 500 collection items
  • 2020: Began virtual tours
  • 2023: Hit 1 million visitors!
